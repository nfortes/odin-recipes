Pompompurin Bubble Tea

Description

A refreshing, Pompompurin themed mango boba.

source

Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/2 c mango (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1/2 c milk
  • 2 Tbsp tapioca pearls (boba)
  • Milk chocolate melting chocolates

Steps

  1. Let's start by piping Pompompurin's face on the glass! Melt down a few of your melting chocolates in a microwave safe bowl for approximately 2 minutes, stopping every 30 seconds to stir. Next, scoop your melted chocolate into a piping bag with tip trim about 1/8 of an inch. Grab your glass and begin by piping Pompompurin's hat about a 1/4 of the way down. Moving down to to the middle of the glass, pipe two small circular eyes. Finish his face with a cute little smile and nose just below his eyes. Set aside.
  2. Now let's move onto the delicious bubble tea! Start by cooking your tapioca pearls. Bring 4 cups of water to a boil and slowly add your tapioca pearls. When the tapioca pearls float to the top of the water, cover the pot and let simmer for 2-3 minutes. Next, remove the tapioca pearls and place in cool water for about 5 minutes. Once cooled, pour into your Pompompurin glass!
  3. Now we're ready for the mango milk! Put your mango, honey, and milk into a blender. Blend ingredients until completely smooth. Next pour into your Pompompurin glass and add ice if desired. Enjoy your refreshing drink!
